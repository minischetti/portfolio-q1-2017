|An Initiative With Impact

Delivering the message was crucial; through education is a solution. A solution to challenges spanning the globe across various fields, from medicine to agriculture and beyond. Created in partnership with Bisk Education, The Mandela Washington Fellowship and The University of Notre Dame, this website provides an introductory experience to The Business of Good Film and the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI), a program started by the Obama administration in 2014.

|The Challenge

To take the original Mandela Washington Fellowship website and transform it into a fresh, modern experience. I designed and developed the website while working closely with a copywriter and stakeholders to ensure the goals of explaining the film, Mandela Washington Fellowship and Young African Leaders Initiative were met.
