|Metro
|Purpose

Metro for Steam is a reimagination of Valve's Steam client and streamlines the original design to feature a cleaner, more functional user interface. With a community group of nearly 30,000 members and over 1,000,000 downloads, it's safe to say Metro has picked up quite a lot of steam (pun intended)!

|A State of Perpetual Motion

Ever-evolving, Metro takes cues from user feedback and modern design practices to stay fresh, while never underestimating the importance of functionality.
